GCS 8-12 = Somnolent
>12 Mild
12-9 Moderate
Somnolence: sleepy, easy to wake
Stupor: hypnoid, hard to wake
GCS < 8 = Unconscious
8-7 Coma grade I Light coma
6-5 Coma grade II
4 Coma grade III Deep coma
3 Coma grade IV
Coma grade I: directed defensive movements, normal tone, no impairment of pupillary and eye movements, vestibular ocular reflex (VOR) positive
II: undirected defensive movements, normal to increased tone, reaction to light intact, pupils variable
III: undirected movements, increased tone, generalized extension and flexion, pupils variable (mostly contracted, unequal), reaction to light decreased, VOR impaired
IV: No reaction to pain, flabby tone, pupils dilated and fixed, VOR negative, craniocaudal loss of brainstem reflexes
