Multiple Sclerosis - Simplified Kurtzke Expanded Disability Status Scale (EDSS)

Score Description
0 Normal
1 Signs, but no disability
2 Minimal disability*
3 Moderate disability,* but able to walk
4 Relatively severe disability,* but able to walk up to 500 m, up to 12 h a day
5 Walking limited to 200 m, unable to do a full day's work without special help
6 Uses walking aid, limited to 100 m
7 Wheelchair bound, able to transfer in and out of chair
8 Bedbound, some arm function
9 Helpless and bedbound
10 Dead

* in other functions: motor, cerebellar, brainstem, sensory, bowel and bladder, visual
